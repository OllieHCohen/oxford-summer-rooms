How it works

A simple 5-step process to book your Oxford summer room.

  1. 1
    🛏️ Choose a room
    Browse the rooms and click “Book this room” on the one you like.
  2. 2
    📅 Choose your dates
    Pick your check-in and check-out from the room’s available summer dates.
  3. 3
    📝 Enter your details
    Add your name, contact details and billing address.
